## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips from Top Engineers

### 1. **Show, Don't Tell**
> "Instead of saying 'it's fast,' show the actual metrics."

### 2. **Be Honest About Limitations**
> "Transparency builds trust. Show what works, acknowledge what doesn't."

### 3. **Make It Easy to Try**
> "One-click demo > 10-step setup. Always."

### 4. **Document Everything**
> "If you can't explain it, you don't understand it."

### 5. **Measure Everything**
> "What gets measured gets improved."

### 6. **Think Long-Term**
> "Build for scale, even if you don't need it yet."

### 7. **Open Source Early**
> "Community feedback > Perfect code in isolation."

### 8. **Automate Everything**
> "Manual processes = errors = lost credibility."
